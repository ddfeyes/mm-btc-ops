FAQ

Straight answers.

Is this your capital or ours?

Yours. We never pool, custody or move your funds. The MM engine runs on your own exchange accounts using your exchange-side inventory and capital. We provide the operations, not the money.

Why is the price indexed to BTC?

It keeps pricing transparent and tied to a public, verifiable number instead of an opaque “custom quote”. Our standard full-service rate is $3,500 per exchange. This summer, while BTC trades in a lower band, you can lock a lower rate for your entire term.

What exactly does “lock the band” mean?

When you prepay a term, your monthly fee is fixed at the band that BTC is in on the day you commit. If BTC climbs into a higher band the next week, your invoiced rate does not change for the duration of your contract.

Do you promise trading volume or price action?

No. We make no price, volume or market-outcome promises. You are paying for a professionally operated, monitored market-making engine and the operational discipline around it — nothing more, nothing less.

What's the difference between the three levels?

Full is the complete operations layer (dashboard, priority response, proactive optimization) at the standard $3,500. Core adds the mini-app, daily reporting and parameter work. Basic is the lean, autonomous, low-touch level for teams that need less coverage.

Can I cover multiple exchanges?

Yes. The rate is per exchange, per month. Set the number of exchanges in the calculator to see your blended monthly and contract totals.

Are there volume or term discounts?

Yes, and they stack. Prepay 2 months for −5% or 3 months for −15% (3 months is the maximum term). Each additional exchange beyond the first is another −5%. Both discounts apply on top of the summer band rate, up to a combined cap of −40%.
